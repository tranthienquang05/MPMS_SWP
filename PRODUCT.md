# Product

## Register

product

## Users

MPMS_SWP serves Admin, Mangaka, Assistant, Tantou editor, and Editor board users who work inside a manga production management workflow. They need to review tasks, manage chapters/pages, communicate, approve work, track status, and move quickly between role-specific operational screens.

## Product Purpose

The product centralizes manga production operations across roles: account management, project/chapter/page tracking, task assignment, submissions, voting/approval, drawing support, notifications, and internal chat. Success means users can understand status at a glance, find the next action quickly, and complete repetitive production tasks without visual noise.

## Brand Personality

Minimal, serious, administrative. The SANKYUU identity should keep its red/black core while feeling more polished, trustworthy, and production-ready.

## Anti-references

Avoid decorative marketing-page styling, playful oversized graphics, excessive gradients, gimmicky animations, and UI that distracts from tables, forms, approvals, and task workflows. Avoid changing interaction patterns that users already rely on.

## Design Principles

Prioritize task clarity over decoration.
Use one consistent component vocabulary across all roles.
Keep the red accent meaningful for primary actions, active states, warnings, and brand identity.
Make dense information easier to scan with spacing, contrast, and hierarchy.
Use motion only for state feedback, notification clarity, and lightweight transitions.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target readable contrast for dark product UI, visible keyboard focus, reduced-motion support, and clear state colors that do not depend on color alone.
